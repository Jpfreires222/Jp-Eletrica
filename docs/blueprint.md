# **App Name**: JP Electric Solutions

## Core Features:

- Service Listing: Display a comprehensive list of electrical services offered by JP Eletrica, including maintenance, installations, and repairs.
- Contact Form: Allow clients to easily submit inquiries and service requests through a simple and effective form.
- Image Gallery: Showcase completed projects and highlight the quality of JP Eletrica's work.
- Testimonials Section: Incorporate customer testimonials to build trust and credibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant electric blue (#29ABE2) to convey energy, trustworthiness, and professionalism, aligning with the electrical services provided. Influenced by electrical colors in art.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F0F0), offering a neutral backdrop that makes the blue pop while keeping the overall feel clean and modern.
- Accent color: Yellow (#FFDA63), reminiscent of electrical sparks, adding visual interest and highlighting key interactive elements such as buttons.
- Font pairing: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for both headings and body text. 'Inter' provides clarity, modernity, and readability which are essential for conveying professionalism.
- Use simple, clear icons related to electrical services, such as a lightbulb, plug, or outlet, in the accent yellow color (#FFDA63).
- Clean, grid-based layout with plenty of white space to ensure readability and a professional appearance.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
